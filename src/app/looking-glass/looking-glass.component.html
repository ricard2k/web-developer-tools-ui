Looking glass requests

With this tool you can see the information your browser are sending for a request, usefull to see and understand the headers, request parameters and other information that are being sent to the server.

Ypu also can use it to simulate a system redirecting to other page. In this case you can include a final part in the URL: /SUCCESS, to indicate the system that the operation was successful, or /ERROR, to indicate that the operation failed. Also you can use /PAID to indicate that a payment was made. https://rick.tools/#/lg/SUCCESS|ERROR|PAID
